Monarch Styling Foundation

Who We Are

The Monarch Styling Foundation is a collective of professional barbers, tattoo artists, and beauty stylists dedicated to opening doors within the trade industries. We recognize that talent is everywhere, but opportunity is often limited by financial barriers. By leveraging our expertise and industry networks, we act as a bridge for aspiring creators who are ready to transform their passion into a career.

Our Mission

"To empower aspiring beauty and body art professionals by providing accessible vocational education and financial assistance. Through dedicated scholarship programs and community resources, we strive to eliminate obstacles like high tuition and lack of transportation."

We believe everyone should have access to education at a low cost. Our focus is specifically on supporting those who are not currently financially stable, providing the tools and training necessary to achieve long-term independence through the styling arts.
