Scholarship Program

Education Without Barriers

At the Monarch Styling Foundation, we believe your financial history shouldn't dictate your professional future. Our scholarship program is specifically designed for aspiring stylists who are currently facing financial instability. We provide tuition assistance that covers the cost of training, kits, and supplies, allowing you to focus entirely on your education.

We also understand that getting to class can be a challenge. That is why we offer dedicated transportation assistance for our students. Whether it’s coordinating transit passes or organizing local carpools, we are committed to making sure that if you have the will to learn, you have a way to get there.
